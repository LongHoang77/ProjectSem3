About ABCD Mall

Our Story

ABCD Mall, situated in the suburban area of Mumbai, is a premier shopping destination developed by ABCD Developers Pvt Ltd. With a sprawling area of 250,000 sq ft (23,000 m¬≤) and a frontage of 700 ft (210 m), our mall stands as a testament to modern retail excellence.

Designed by Cooper and their partners, ABCD Mall features:

  • 4-storeyed air-conditioned mall
  • 80,000 sq ft (7,000 m¬≤) retail space per floor
  • 7-level car parking facility
  • Modern architecture and design
